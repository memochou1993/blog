## 步驟

修改 `_config.yml` 檔：

```yaml
new_post_name: :year/:year-:month-:day-:title.md # File name of new posts
```

修改 `scaffolds\draft.md` 檔：

```md
---
title: {{ title }}
date: {{ date }}
tags:
categories:
---
```

- 新增文章時，將 permalink 標題的空格改為 `-` 符號。
